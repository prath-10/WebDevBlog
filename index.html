Custom web design vs Template website
What to choose?

Template Website


The most ordinary method to start a website would be to set up a Wix/WordPress/ Squarespace site and apply a template. It just may be the easiest and most flexible blogging and website content management system (CMS) for beginners. These websites provide a broad range of different types of website templates available for purchase or free depending the theme. These can help you get a website up pretty fast and easily.



Custom Web Design


A custom-built website is generated from scratch. It is coded according to whatever your need or your business requirements. Custom built websites involve a individual/team behind your business. It starts with a creative process to understand who is your target audience, who do you want to reach, how you want/need the website to function and how do you want to look on the internet.


Pros of a template website


• If your budget is tight, then this might the option you should be going with as it save's you some money.
• Save a lot of time as the templates are pre-built to meet certain common functionality requirements.
• Easy to make your own as no coding is required.
• SEO optimisation.



Cons of a template website


• The additional support you get with a template theme is generally very slow. It can take weeks to find solutions.
• There can be restrictions and plugin conflicts if you want to customize the theme.
• Potential design compromises
• The build is often bloated with extra code and so it runs slower than a custom theme.



Pros of a custom website


• Fewer limitations, as when you’re investing in a new website for your business, the last thing you want to do is compromise.
• You will get all necessary features.
• Very customisable.
• Custom functionality.



Cons of a custom website


• Custom design and development is a major time investment.
• More expensive.
• Any changes to the site may take time.
• You will have to rely on your developers time and availability.



What to Choose?

Both a template and a custom-made solution can properly handle business challenges.
The presence of an expensive exclusive design is not a guarantee that users will immediately love it.
But an exclusive project allows you to improve user experience through a unique design and a high level of usability. This option is better suited for those who know their target audience well and want to create a website for it.
